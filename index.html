How to read this: Baseline = €0 (keeping current car). Positive = you are richer.
EV age factor: Maintenance & repairs grow +8%/yr above CPI. Current car grows +12%/yr (older ICE unreliability penalty).
Depreciation: Front-loaded convex curve (t^1.6) — matches observed used EV market data (AutoTrader PT, Autobild 2023-25).
Battery degradation: Electricity consumption rises each year per per-car degrad rate. Leaf (no active thermal) degrades fastest (2.8%/yr).
CGT: 28% applied at year 10 on QDVE portfolio gain only (not total NW delta). Matches Portuguese ETF redemption tax law.
Public charging: Effective kWh cost = weighted blend of home and public rates. Adjusted annually for battery degradation.
Alt1 loan shortfall: If savings < loan payment in early years, shortfall assumed paid from income (not additional QDVE withdrawal).
Sensitivity band: ±20% on gross investment return — recomputed at different return rates (not scaled savings).
⚠ Leaf & Zoe: Thin resale market in Portugal. Residual10 values may be optimistic — consider applying a manual haircut in the override fields.
Bugs fixed vs prior Python versions: Residual delta (was double-counted — up to €100K error at yr10), CGT base (QDVE gain only), CGT parity Alt1/Alt3, R_base sensitivity (recomputed, not scaled), Alt2 initial withdrawal, summary table (now computed).
